notes

  • any content word (a word with a noun, verb, or modifier definition) can be used as either a noun, verb, or modifier, even if no definition for one of those parts of speech is in this dictionary.
  • numbers are content words.
  • any word with a noun definition can be used as a verb to mean "to be [noun definition]".
