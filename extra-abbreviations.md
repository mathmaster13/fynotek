| Abbreviation | Meaning |
| ------------ | ------- |
| *rñ* (rarely *yrñ*) | *ratasñy* (similar to "idk" in English) |
| *a*/*k* | *ak*/*ka* |
| X*k*Y | X *kynsi* Y (used for times; see the [Dates and Times](INSERT LINK) lesson) |
